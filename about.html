DNCE

About

DNCE is an American dance-rock band that gained popularity with their catchy and energetic music. Here's a quick overview: Formation: The band was formed in 2015. It's fronted by lead singer Joe Jonas. Other members include drummer Jack Lawless and guitarist JinJoo Lee. Former member Cole Whittle. Music Style: Their music blends dance-rock, dance-pop, pop rock, and funk-pop. Key Hits: They are best known for their hit single "Cake by the Ocean." Activity: They released their self-titled debut album in 2016. They have had periods of activity and hiatus. They returned in 2022, and then went back on hiatus in mid 2023.

Origins and Formation: The band's inception came about when Joe Jonas and Jack Lawless were living together. The project was initially delayed due to their busy schedules. The name "DNCE" comes from a song they wrote, symbolizing the "imperfect awesomeness" of the group. Musical Style and Influences: Their sound is a fusion of dance-rock, dance-pop, pop-rock, and funk-pop. Influences include artists like Electric Light Orchestra, Sly and the Family Stone, and Prince, contributing to their diverse and energetic sound. Key Releases and Achievements: "Cake by the Ocean" was a massive hit, propelling them to mainstream success. Their self-titled debut album and EPs showcased their versatility. They've received award nominations and wins, including an MTV Video Music Award for Best New Artist. Band Member Backgrounds: Joe Jonas's prior fame with the Jonas Brothers provided a strong foundation. Jack Lawless had a history of drumming for the Jonas Brothers. JinJoo Lee has toured with many famous artist. Cole Whittle has a background in the band Semi Precious weapons. Periods of Activity: The band has experienced periods of activity and hiatus, with a notable return in 2022. They have recently gone back on hiatus in mid 2023.
